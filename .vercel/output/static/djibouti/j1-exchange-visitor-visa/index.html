J1 Exchange Visitor Visa Requirements
for Djibouti Citizens

Requirements for Djibouti citizens applying for a US J1 Exchange Visitor Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of Djibouti applying for a J1 Exchange Visitor Visa must expect a rigorous screening process, including a thorough review of their application and supporting documents, as well as a mandatory interview at the US Embassy in Djibouti. Citizens of Djibouti who are granted a J1 Exchange Visitor Visa are required to maintain health insurance that meets specific US Department of State requirements for the duration of their stay in the United States.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (min. 6 months validity beyond stay)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application (completed)
  • ✓MRV visa application fee payment receipt ($185)
  • ✓Form DS-2019 (Certificate of Eligibility) from a designated sponsor
  • ✓SEVIS I-901 fee payment receipt
  • ✓One recent passport-style photograph
  • ✓Proof of financial support for the exchange program
  • ✓Sponsor program letter and details

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa ValidityVaries; check US Embassy website

ℹ️ Important Notes

Note: Citizens of Djibouti must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
