H1B Work Visa Requirements
for Rwanda Citizens

Requirements for Rwanda citizens applying for a US H1B Work Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of Rwanda applying for a H1B Work Visa must expect a lengthy and highly competitive application process that requires a sponsoring US employer and adherence to strict eligibility criteria. Citizens of Rwanda who are granted a H1B Work Visa are authorized to work in the United States for a maximum period of six years, with the option to apply for an extension or change of status under specific circumstances.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application
  • ✓Visa application fee payment receipt
  • ✓Photo meeting US visa requirements
  • ✓Interview appointment confirmation

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa ValidityUp to 10 years (B1/B2); varies by visa type

ℹ️ Important Notes

Note: Citizens of Rwanda must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
