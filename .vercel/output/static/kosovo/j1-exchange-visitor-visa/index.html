J1 Exchange Visitor Visa Requirements
for Kosovo Citizens

Requirements for Kosovo citizens applying for a US J1 Exchange Visitor Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of Kosovo applying for a J1 Exchange Visitor Visa must expect a mandatory two-year home-country physical presence requirement after completing their program in the United States. Citizens of Kosovo who are eligible for the J1 Exchange Visitor Visa can participate in various exchange programs, including internships, trainings, and research collaborations, for a maximum duration of 18 months to 3 years, depending on the specific program category.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (min. 6 months validity beyond stay)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application (completed)
  • ✓MRV visa application fee payment receipt ($185)
  • ✓Form DS-2019 (Certificate of Eligibility) from a designated sponsor
  • ✓SEVIS I-901 fee payment receipt
  • ✓One recent passport-style photograph
  • ✓Proof of financial support for the exchange program
  • ✓Sponsor program letter and details

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa ValidityVaries; check US Embassy website

ℹ️ Important Notes

Note: Citizens of Kosovo must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
