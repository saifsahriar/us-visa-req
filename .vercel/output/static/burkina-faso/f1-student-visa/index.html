F1 Student Visa Requirements
for Burkina Faso Citizens

Requirements for Burkina Faso citizens applying for a US F1 Student Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of Burkina Faso applying for a F1 Student Visa must expect to provide proof of sufficient financial resources to support themselves for the duration of their studies in the United States. Citizens of Burkina Faso who are granted a F1 Student Visa are required to pursue a full course of study at an approved institution and maintain a valid visa status to remain in the United States.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (min. 6 months validity beyond stay)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application (completed)
  • ✓MRV visa application fee payment receipt ($185)
  • ✓Form I-20 (Certificate of Eligibility) from a SEVP-accredited school
  • ✓SEVIS I-901 fee payment receipt
  • ✓One recent passport-style photograph
  • ✓Proof of financial ability to cover tuition and living expenses
  • ✓Academic transcripts and diplomas

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa ValidityVaries; check US Embassy website

ℹ️ Important Notes

Note: Citizens of Burkina Faso must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
