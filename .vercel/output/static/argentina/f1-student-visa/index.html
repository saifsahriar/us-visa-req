F1 Student Visa Requirements
for Argentina Citizens

Requirements for Argentina citizens applying for a US F1 Student Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of Argentina applying for a F1 Student Visa must expect to provide proof of sufficient financial resources to support themselves during their stay in the United States. Citizens of Argentina who are granted a F1 Student Visa are required to pursue a full course of study at an approved institution and maintain a valid passport throughout their stay in the United States.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (min. 6 months validity beyond stay)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application (completed)
  • ✓MRV visa application fee payment receipt ($185)
  • ✓Form I-20 (Certificate of Eligibility) from a SEVP-accredited school
  • ✓SEVIS I-901 fee payment receipt
  • ✓One recent passport-style photograph
  • ✓Proof of financial ability to cover tuition and living expenses
  • ✓Academic transcripts and diplomas

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa Validity22 Months

ℹ️ Important Notes

Note: Citizens of Argentina must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
