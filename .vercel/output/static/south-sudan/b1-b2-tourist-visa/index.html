B1/B2 Tourist Visa Requirements
for South Sudan Citizens

Requirements for South Sudan citizens applying for a US B1/B2 Tourist Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of South Sudan applying for a B1/B2 Tourist Visa must expect a rigorous interview process at the US embassy, where they will be required to demonstrate strong ties to their home country and a clear intention to return after their temporary stay in the United States. Citizens of South Sudan are required to submit their B1/B2 Tourist Visa applications in person at the US Embassy in Juba, as there are no other authorized application submission locations available for South Sudanese nationals.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (min. 6 months validity beyond stay)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application (completed)
  • ✓MRV visa application fee payment receipt ($185)
  • ✓One recent passport-style photograph
  • ✓Interview appointment confirmation letter
  • ✓Proof of strong ties to home country (employment letter, property deeds, family)
  • ✓Proof of financial ability to cover travel expenses (bank statements)

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa ValidityVaries; check US Embassy website

ℹ️ Important Notes

Note: Citizens of South Sudan must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
