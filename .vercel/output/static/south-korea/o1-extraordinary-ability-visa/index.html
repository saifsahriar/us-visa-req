O1 Extraordinary Ability Visa Requirements
for South Korea Citizens

Requirements for South Korea citizens applying for a US O1 Extraordinary Ability Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of South Korea applying for a O1 Extraordinary Ability Visa must demonstrate extraordinary ability in their field through sustained national or international acclaim, as evidenced by extensive documentation and supporting materials. Citizens of South Korea who are granted a O1 Extraordinary Ability Visa are permitted to work in the United States for a specific period, typically up to three years, with the possibility of extension, in order to continue working in their field of extraordinary ability.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application
  • ✓Visa application fee payment receipt
  • ✓Photo meeting US visa requirements
  • ✓Interview appointment confirmation

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa ValidityUp to 10 years (B1/B2); varies by visa type

ℹ️ Important Notes

Note: Citizens of South Korea must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
