L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa Requirements
for Syria Citizens

Requirements for Syria citizens applying for a US L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of Syria applying for a L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa must expect a rigorous review process due to the US government's heightened security measures for applicants from Syria. Citizens of Syria are eligible to apply for a L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa, but they must meet the standard eligibility requirements, including having worked for the company outside the US for at least one year and being employed in a managerial, executive, or specialized knowledge capacity.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application
  • ✓Visa application fee payment receipt
  • ✓Photo meeting US visa requirements
  • ✓Interview appointment confirmation

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa ValidityUp to 10 years (B1/B2); varies by visa type

Note: Citizens of Syria must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
