H1B Work Visa Requirements
for Syria Citizens

Requirements for Syria citizens applying for a US H1B Work Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of Syria applying for a H1B Work Visa must expect a higher level of scrutiny and additional administrative processing due to the US Department of State's designation of Syria as a State Sponsor of Terrorism. Citizens of Syria who are granted a H1B Work Visa are typically subject to strict requirements and limitations, including a requirement to apply for a visa at a US embassy or consulate outside of Syria, as the US Embassy in Damascus has been closed since 2012.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application
  • ✓Visa application fee payment receipt
  • ✓Photo meeting US visa requirements
  • ✓Interview appointment confirmation

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa ValidityUp to 10 years (B1/B2); varies by visa type

ℹ️ Important Notes

Note: Citizens of Syria must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
