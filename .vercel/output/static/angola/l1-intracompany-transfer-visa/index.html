L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa Requirements
for Angola Citizens

Requirements for Angola citizens applying for a US L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of Angola applying for a L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa must expect a rigorous application process that requires detailed documentation of their employment history and qualifications with a US-based company or its subsidiary. Citizens of Angola who are granted a L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa are permitted to work in the United States for a maximum initial period of one year, with potential extensions available for up to a total of seven years for specialized knowledge employees or five years for executive or managerial employees.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (min. 6 months validity beyond stay)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application (completed)
  • ✓MRV visa application fee payment receipt ($185)
  • ✓Form I-797 Notice of Action (USCIS petition approval notice)
  • ✓One recent passport-style photograph
  • ✓Employer support letter describing the intracompany transfer
  • ✓Proof of employment with foreign company for at least 1 year in last 3 years
  • ✓Evidence of managerial, executive, or specialized knowledge role

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa Validity3 Months

ℹ️ Important Notes

Note: Citizens of Angola must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
