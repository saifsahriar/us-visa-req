L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa Requirements
for Monaco Citizens

Requirements for Monaco citizens applying for a US L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of Monaco applying for a L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa must expect to demonstrate a qualifying relationship between the US company and the foreign company, typically through ownership or control. Citizens of Monaco are eligible to apply for a L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa, which allows them to work in the US for a subsidiary, affiliate, or parent company of their current employer in Monaco, provided they meet specific requirements and qualifications.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (min. 6 months validity beyond stay)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application (completed)
  • ✓MRV visa application fee payment receipt ($185)
  • ✓Form I-797 Notice of Action (USCIS petition approval notice)
  • ✓One recent passport-style photograph
  • ✓Employer support letter describing the intracompany transfer
  • ✓Proof of employment with foreign company for at least 1 year in last 3 years
  • ✓Evidence of managerial, executive, or specialized knowledge role

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa Validity60 Months

ℹ️ Important Notes

Note: Citizens of Monaco must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
