B1/B2 Tourist Visa Requirements
for Zimbabwe Citizens

Requirements for Zimbabwe citizens applying for a US B1/B2 Tourist Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of Zimbabwe applying for a B1/B2 Tourist Visa must expect to participate in a mandatory interview at the US Embassy in Harare as part of the application process. Citizens of Zimbabwe are required to submit a completed DS-160 application form and supporting documents, including proof of strong ties to Zimbabwe, to be considered for a B1/B2 Tourist Visa for temporary travel to the United States.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (min. 6 months validity beyond stay)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application (completed)
  • ✓MRV visa application fee payment receipt ($185)
  • ✓One recent passport-style photograph
  • ✓Interview appointment confirmation letter
  • ✓Proof of strong ties to home country (employment letter, property deeds, family)
  • ✓Proof of financial ability to cover travel expenses (bank statements)

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa Validity3 Months

ℹ️ Important Notes

Note: Citizens of Zimbabwe must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
