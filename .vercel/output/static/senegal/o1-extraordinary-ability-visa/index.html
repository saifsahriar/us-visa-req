O1 Extraordinary Ability Visa Requirements
for Senegal Citizens

Requirements for Senegal citizens applying for a US O1 Extraordinary Ability Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of Senegal applying for a O1 Extraordinary Ability Visa must expect to provide extensive documentation demonstrating their extraordinary ability in their field, including evidence of awards, publications, and membership in associations requiring outstanding achievements. Citizens of Senegal who are granted a O1 Extraordinary Ability Visa are permitted to work in the United States for a specific period, typically up to three years, with the possibility of extensions, and must maintain their nonimmigrant status by continuing to work in their field of extraordinary ability.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (min. 6 months validity beyond stay)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application (completed)
  • ✓MRV visa application fee payment receipt ($185)
  • ✓Form I-797 Notice of Action (USCIS petition approval notice)
  • ✓One recent passport-style photograph
  • ✓Evidence of extraordinary ability (awards, publications, press, salary evidence)
  • ✓Written advisory opinion from a peer group or expert
  • ✓US agent or employer contract or itinerary of events

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa Validity3 Months3

Note: Citizens of Senegal must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
