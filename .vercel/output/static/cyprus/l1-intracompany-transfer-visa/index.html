L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa Requirements
for Cyprus Citizens

Requirements for Cyprus citizens applying for a US L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of Cyprus applying for a L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa must expect to demonstrate a minimum of one year of continuous employment with the petitioning company outside the United States within the three years preceding their admission. Citizens of Cyprus are eligible to apply for a L1 Intracompany Transfer Visa if they are being transferred to a US-based office of the same company in an executive, managerial, or specialized knowledge capacity.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (min. 6 months validity beyond stay)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application (completed)
  • ✓MRV visa application fee payment receipt ($185)
  • ✓Form I-797 Notice of Action (USCIS petition approval notice)
  • ✓One recent passport-style photograph
  • ✓Employer support letter describing the intracompany transfer
  • ✓Proof of employment with foreign company for at least 1 year in last 3 years
  • ✓Evidence of managerial, executive, or specialized knowledge role

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa Validity48 Months

ℹ️ Important Notes

Note: Citizens of Cyprus must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
