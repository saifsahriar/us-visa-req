J1 Exchange Visitor Visa Requirements
for Gambia Citizens

Requirements for Gambia citizens applying for a US J1 Exchange Visitor Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of Gambia applying for a J1 Exchange Visitor Visa must expect to participate in a program that promotes cultural exchange and learning, such as internship, traineeship, or research opportunities, for a specific period of time. Citizens of Gambia who are granted a J1 Exchange Visitor Visa are required to return to their home country for at least two years after completing their program, unless they obtain a waiver of the two-year foreign residence requirement.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (min. 6 months validity beyond stay)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application (completed)
  • ✓MRV visa application fee payment receipt ($185)
  • ✓Form DS-2019 (Certificate of Eligibility) from a designated sponsor
  • ✓SEVIS I-901 fee payment receipt
  • ✓One recent passport-style photograph
  • ✓Proof of financial support for the exchange program
  • ✓Sponsor program letter and details

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa ValidityVaries; check US Embassy website

ℹ️ Important Notes

Note: Citizens of Gambia must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
