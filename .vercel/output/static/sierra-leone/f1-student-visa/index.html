F1 Student Visa Requirements
for Sierra Leone Citizens

Requirements for Sierra Leone citizens applying for a US F1 Student Visa.

📌 Visa Overview

Citizens of Sierra Leone applying for a F1 Student Visa must expect to provide proof of sufficient financial resources to support themselves for the duration of their studies in the United States. Citizens of Sierra Leone who are granted an F1 Student Visa are required to pursue a full course of study at an approved institution and maintain a valid passport for the duration of their stay in the United States.

📋 Required Documents

  • ✓Valid passport (min. 6 months validity beyond stay)
  • ✓DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application (completed)
  • ✓MRV visa application fee payment receipt ($185)
  • ✓Form I-20 (Certificate of Eligibility) from a SEVP-accredited school
  • ✓SEVIS I-901 fee payment receipt
  • ✓One recent passport-style photograph
  • ✓Proof of financial ability to cover tuition and living expenses
  • ✓Academic transcripts and diplomas

📊 Key Details

Processing Time3-5 business days after interview
Application Fee💰 $185 USD
Visa ValidityVaries; check US Embassy website

ℹ️ Important Notes

Note: Citizens of Sierra Leone must apply at the nearest US Embassy or Consulate.
